# samples/

Drop 3–5 of your own long-form pieces here before running BOOTSTRAP mode.

- Plain `.md` or `.txt`, one piece per file.
- Anything representative of your voice: published blog posts, essays, internal memos, long Slack posts. Avoid tweets or one-liners — the skill needs rhythm and paragraph structure to extract a fingerprint.
- More ≠ better. 3 strong samples beat 10 mediocre ones.

Your samples are personal. This folder is gitignored in the source repo and not copied back out by the installer.
